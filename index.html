About College

Er. G. Ponnusamy’s commitment to accessible education extends beyond mere provision; it’s a deeply rooted passion to uplift marginalized communities through knowledge and skill development.

Recognizing the critical need for vocational training, he established the ITI in 1979, laying the foundation for countless individuals to acquire practical skills and secure meaningful employment. This initial step was followed by the establishment of Tirupattur Arts and Science College, GP Polytechnic, and GP College of Education, each strategically designed to cater to diverse educational needs and empower students across various disciplines.
